Meridian Engineering Consultants — Service Guide & FAQ

About Meridian Engineering

Meridian Engineering Consultants is a multidisciplinary engineering firm providing structural, civil, and environmental engineering services since 2010. We serve commercial developers, government agencies, and industrial clients. Our team of 25 licensed Professional Engineers (PEs) delivers projects across the region.

Our Services

Structural Engineering:
We design and analyze structures for commercial buildings, industrial facilities, bridges, and retrofits. Services include structural design, seismic analysis, load calculations, foundation design, steel and concrete detailing, and structural inspections. All designs comply with IBC 2021 (International Building Code) and local amendments.

Civil Engineering:
Our civil team handles site development, grading plans, stormwater management, utility design, road design, and land development. We prepare all necessary permit applications and work with local municipalities throughout the approval process. Typical site development projects take 3-6 months from survey to permit approval.

Environmental Engineering:
We provide Phase I and Phase II Environmental Site Assessments (ESAs), contamination remediation design, air quality analysis, noise studies, and environmental impact reports. Phase I ESAs typically take 3-4 weeks and cost between $3,000-$5,000.

Project Management:
For complex multi-discipline projects, we offer full project management including scheduling, budget tracking, contractor coordination, and quality assurance. Our PMs are PMP-certified and use Procore for project tracking.

Our Process

1. Initial Consultation: Free 1-hour meeting to discuss project scope and requirements
2. Proposal: We provide a detailed scope of work, timeline, and fee proposal within 5 business days
3. Design Phase: Engineering design with regular client check-ins at 30%, 60%, and 90% completion
4. Permitting: We handle all permit submissions and respond to review comments
5. Construction Support: Available for site visits, RFI responses, and inspections during construction

Fee Structure

Engineering fees are typically calculated as a percentage of construction cost:
- Structural design: 1-3% of structural construction cost
- Civil/site design: 1.5-4% of site construction cost
- Environmental assessments: Fixed fee (see service list)

For smaller projects, we offer hourly rates: $175-$300/hour depending on engineer seniority.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need a structural engineer for my renovation?
A: If you're removing walls, adding floors, changing roof structure, or modifying load-bearing elements, yes. Even cosmetic renovations may need engineering sign-off depending on your municipality.

Q: How long does a typical commercial building design take?
A: Small commercial (under 10,000 sq ft): 6-10 weeks. Medium (10,000-50,000 sq ft): 10-16 weeks. Large or complex: 16-24+ weeks.

Q: What's included in a Phase I Environmental Assessment?
A: Historical research of the property, regulatory database review, site reconnaissance visit, interviews with current/past owners, and a final report identifying potential environmental concerns. No soil or water sampling (that's Phase II).

Q: Can you help with permit rejections?
A: Yes. We regularly assist clients whose permits were rejected. We'll review the comments, make necessary revisions, and resubmit. Our 95% first-resubmission approval rate speaks to our thorough initial designs.

Q: Do you offer emergency structural assessments?
A: Yes. For building damage from storms, earthquakes, fire, or accidents, we provide 24-hour emergency response. Call our emergency line: (555) 876-5432.
